About Me

Cybersecurity & Legal Professional

Keniz Otieno Agira is a distinguished cybersecurity expert and legal professional with extensive experience in cyber law, digital forensics, and information security. As the Chairman of the Kenya Cybersecurity and Forensics Association (KCSFA), he plays a pivotal role in shaping Kenya's cybersecurity landscape.

With a unique combination of legal expertise and technical knowledge in cybersecurity, Keniz bridges the gap between law and technology, providing comprehensive solutions to digital security challenges. His work focuses on creating secure digital environments while ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory frameworks.

Keniz is recognized for his leadership in fostering collaborations between academic institutions, government agencies, and private sector organizations to enhance cybersecurity capabilities in Kenya and across Africa.
